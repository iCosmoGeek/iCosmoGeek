Average Salaries at Apple and Google

May 31, 2012

Ever wondering if you’d be better off working for Apple or for Google? Who pays better? Apple or Google? Who gets paid more: designers or engineers? What is the average salary of employees at these companies?

A job at either of these tech giants isn’t guaranteed to make you rich, but they do pay their talent fairly well, especially their engineers and designers. Here’s a list of some of the most common positions within Apple and Google, along with their average salary, in ranking order. Business Insider compiled this list in a post from the salary data of Glassdoor, a free jobs and career community that offers the world an inside look at jobs and companies.

More details below:
