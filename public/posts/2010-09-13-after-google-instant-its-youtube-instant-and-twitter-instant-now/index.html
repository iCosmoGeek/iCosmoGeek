After Google Instant, its YouTube Instant and Twitter Instant now

September 13, 2010

Close on heels of Google Instant, here comes YouTube Instant and Twitter Instant, possibly the fastest way to instantly search videos and Twitter right now.

Youtube Instant is the creation of Feross Aboukhadijeh, a computer science student at Stanford University, who bet with his roommate (after the launch of Google Instant) and built a real-time YouTube search in 3 hours!
