Angry Birds for Windows Phone 7 Coming on May 25

April 15, 2011

The much awaited popular iOS and Android platform game Angry Birds is coming now on Windows Phone 7. The news is revealed at the on-going MIX 2011 event.

According to the official announcement, Xbox Live title Angry Birds will be released on 25th of May. Xbox Live was supposed to to start on April 6th but was quietly delayed for reasons unknown. Furthermore, a competent Angry Birds clone “Chicks ‘N Vixens” has stolen the attention of WP7 fans. You can try out this game on your WP7 device here directly from Zune.
